✦ How to Use This Tool

01

Choose your character type first — each type focuses the AI on what matters most for that role. A Mentor gets wisdom and cost; a Villain gets belief and motive; a Wild Card gets chaos and hidden agenda.

02

Set your setting, tone, flaw, and name style using the dropdowns. Mix unexpected combinations — a Cozy Fantasy Villain with Blind Loyalty, or a Rival in a Mythpunk world — for the most interesting results.

03

Pick an age group to ensure the character feels right for your intended audience — vocabulary, emotional complexity, and life stage all adjust accordingly.

04

Hit Generate and get a full concept in seconds. Not in love with the result? Hit Generate Again — same settings, fresh character every time. Tweak one parameter at a time to zero in on exactly what you need.

05

Copy This Concept puts the full character in plain text on your clipboard — ready to paste into your manuscript notes, Scrivener, Google Docs, or anywhere else you work.

06

Need more? This tool gives you the spark. For a complete, client-ready Character Bible with full supporting cast, antagonist, arc, and PDF delivery — see the options below.
